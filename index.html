EnterpriseFlow AI Platform Vision

EnterpriseFlow AI is an advanced agentic operating system designed to automate complex corporate workflows directly within Snowflake database schemas. By integrating Snowflake Cortex Large Language Models, the platform processes data intelligence pipelines, discovers schema anomalies, runs comparative forecasts, and triggers real-time Slack and Jira notifications.

Our mission is to establish a secure, human-in-the-loop governance model for autonomous enterprise agents. High-value data transactions are subject to signature checks and manual authorization steps, ensuring audit transparency and compliance at scale.
